Cory Timmons is an artist and designer based in Salem, OR. Her work is situated at the intersection of video, writing, net art, drag, and collage. Submerged in layers of ambiguity, she swims through her own marginalization in an androcentric, (trans)misogynistic world. Exploring trauma, family systems, gender politics, and corporate excess, she heals wounds and redefines space for the outsiders in society.

She revels in the satisfaction of removing wallpaper, battling fiends in Final Fantasy, laughing maniacally to crass novels, mowing dandelions, and eating chocolate cake. If you bump into her at the thrift store shopping for materials, be sure to say hi, or drop her a line.
